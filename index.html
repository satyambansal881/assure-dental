Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful? +
No. With modern anaesthesia and techniques, root canal treatment at Assure Dental is virtually painless. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable the experience is. Single-sitting RCT is also available.
How long does a dental implant procedure take? +
The implant placement itself usually takes 30–60 minutes per tooth. The full process including healing can take 3–6 months for permanent restoration. We also offer same-day teeth options in select cases.
Do you treat children? +
Absolutely. We have dedicated pediatric dental services and our doctors are known for their gentle, child-friendly approach. We make dental visits a positive experience for kids of all ages.
How much does a dental implant cost in Agra? +
The cost depends on the type of implant and individual case. We offer competitive pricing and transparent cost breakdowns before any procedure. Book a free consultation to get an accurate estimate.
What are your clinic hours? +
We are open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are preferred to minimise wait time.
Do you offer teeth whitening? +
Yes. We offer professional in-clinic teeth whitening that delivers visible results in a single session — far more effective and safer than over-the-counter products.
